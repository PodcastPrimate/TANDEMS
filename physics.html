--- layout: default title: Physics Breakdown nav_order: 2 --- # Extended Physics Breakdown – Project T.A.N.D.E.M.S. RealSimHQ's T.A.N.D.E.M.S. physics are built using the bleeding-edge CSP features. Here's what sets them apart from vanilla AC and AC EVO. ---
• 5-Point Tyre Contact vs 1-Point

What it is: CSP 0.20+ replaces the single-patch tyre model with five independent contact points.

Why it matters: You get realistic transitions over curbs, grass, and bumps—no more “pop” and instant loss of grip.

• Dynamic Chassis Flex & Torsion

What it is: Real-time body/frame torsion under load.

Why it matters: Feel the car twist and settle like in real life, instead of a rigid block.

• Precision FFB Tweaks

What it is: A suite of new force-feedback parameters (damping, smoothing, channel emissives, etc.).

Why it matters: You actually feel weight transfer, tyre scrub, and suspension movement—not just engine torque.

• Cosmic Suspension Physics Engine

What it is: Build your suspension from “atomic” joints—ball joints, sliders, distance joints—with separate spring/damper LUTs.

Why it matters: True-to-life camber change, roll center shifts, anti-dive/squat—all in real time.

• 4 Extra Tyre Sets with Realistic Warming & Wear

What it is: Temperature-based grip curves and progressive degradation.

Why it matters: Predictable lap-to-lap evolution, just like on track.

• Custom Collision Models

What it is: Server-side hooks for ERP, CFM, bounce, friction, intensity—even mass multipliers.

Why it matters: Crash behaviour you can dial in—soft-bounce or ACC-style contacts.

• Exclusive to CSP – AC EVO Can’t Have ‘Em

What it is: Assetto Corsa Evo is a standalone rebuild and doesn’t support CSP or Content Manager mods.

Why it matters: These extended-physics goodies are only available via the paid CSP preview.
