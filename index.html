Benefits of Extended Physics: T.A.N.D.E.M. vs. Standard AC vs. AC Evo

Extended physics in T.A.N.D.E.M. offer nuanced tire model behavior, advanced suspension geometry, and improved force feedback, leading to more predictable and immersive drift control compared to Assetto Corsa's original or Evo physics models. While Assetto Corsa's original physics are foundational, and Evo builds upon them, T.A.N.D.E.M. specifically refines aspects critical for high-performance drifting, such as weight transfer accuracy and slip angle interpretation, allowing for greater precision in tandem scenarios.

Key Comparison Areas (Conceptual)

  • **Tire Model Fidelity:** How accurately the tire interacts with the surface (grip, slip, heat).
  • **Weight Transfer Accuracy:** How vehicle weight shifts during transitions and its impact on grip.
  • **Force Feedback Realism:** The quality and informative nature of FFB.
  • **Predictability at Limit:** How controllable the car is at its grip limit.
  • **Setup Depth:** The extent to which car setups can influence behavior.
